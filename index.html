ABOUT

Filmed by Madeline Hicks 2014

Molly Mintz is studying Journalism + Design and Global Studies at The New School in Manhattan. Her interests in crime, security, and injustice; fashion, art, and culture; women's and human rights; politics; and the environment have propelled her into a multitude of different fields. With a creative passion for perpetuating societal change, Molly strives to grow as a journalist.
